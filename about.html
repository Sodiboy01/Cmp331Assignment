Federal University, Dutsin-Ma is a federal university located in kastina state south west Nigeria. The university was founded in 2011 and approved by NUC to operate as a federal university. The university has no religious or cultural affiliation and enrolls students without using either of these background as criteria for enrolment. The university offers cheap tuition and has a relatively diverse student body in terms of religion, age and other values. Federal University, Dutsin-Ma courses are in areas cutting across sciences and social sciences. Admission into this university is not so competitive. We don't have figures of the university's student population but we believe it would have a large student body. Federal University, Dutsin-Ma school fees [N100,000] is affordable being a federal university and thus being funded by the federal government. it is well developed in term of infrastructure, hotels and college buildings. Students live on campus and off campus.
